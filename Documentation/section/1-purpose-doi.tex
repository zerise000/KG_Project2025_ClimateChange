
\section{Project Design}

The purpose of this project is to engineer a Knowledge Graph which models various meteorological
facilities, and, thereby, models the climate  in the Trentino region
during the decade 2015-2025 in order to get a complete view of the following informations:
 what are the most impacted area by the climate change
 where the major weather stations are located 
 and how the wheater and the temperature distributions evolved
